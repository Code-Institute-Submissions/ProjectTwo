Poor state funding for education

School did not always come easy to William, but thanks to the perseverance of his mother, and the dedication of his teachers, they finally named the problem – dyslexia. His dyslexia led him on a unique educational journey, one of self-pace and self-discovery. It’s what he needed to help him learn and it’s shaped his thinking today: William believes that every student in Nevada deserves the same opportunity to achieve success. As Governor, William is calling for the Nevada Promise, a new way of thinking about education as a lifelong pursuit. He believes that our role begins when babies are still in the womb and it doesn’t end until we’ve done all we can to prepare them for a quality job and successful career.

The state of Nevada has a large problem which the current administration has not appeared to resolve, The education level of this beautiful state is in dire straits. As you can see from data acquired by the donorsUSA panel, Nevada is very much in the lower half of this great country and it is in my opinion its due to the education failure of our schools that must be addressed.
